Glamfame uses cookies on its platform to obtain information about its visitors in order to upgrade customer satisfaction and keep website functionality at high levels. The cookie is a small portion of data, which stays in touch with your PC or mobile device and records information about users' activities, such as interests, preferences, etc. There are mainly two types of cookies – session and persistent ones. The first type of cookies expires when the user closes the web session on Glamfame and aims to show the user relevant information on the website, while the persistent ones stick on his/her browser for a certain period after the end of the browsing session (e.g. 15 days, or 30 days) and aims to show the user ads and information that corresponds to his/her preferences. See also our Cookie Privacy Policy below.
